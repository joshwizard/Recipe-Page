Sauteed Omelette Recipe

In the 21st Century, the most nutrient dense diet to have on your table is none other than
Sauteed Omelette. It is one of the most preferred meal in 100% of all the 5 to 7 star hotels.
Follow the underlisted steps to cook a mouth watering meal that will make your neighbours neglect their food and knock on your door for a bite.

Ingredients

  • 5 eggs & 5 tablespoons of water
  • 1 brocolli, 1 bell pepper & 5 leaves of spring onion
  • Rosemary, coriander leaves & garlic
  • Coconut oil & Extra Virgin Olive oil
  • Salt & Tumeric powder

Instructions

Step 1

  1. Wash broccoli, bell pepper, spring onion, rosemary, coriander and garlic with clean running water.
  2. Pluck broccoli buds and slice the bell pepper, spring onion, rosemary, coriander & garlic in the chopping board into tiny pieces.
  3. Pour these pieces into a grinder/blender/shredder and shred them into tinier finer pieces.
  4. Put these shredded vegetable pieces into a wet shallow pan and steam them over a light fire for 10 minutes to make pottage
  5. Sprinkle 2 tablespoons of olive oil over the pottage and then remove this steamed vegetable pottage and set
    it aside in a bowl. Cover it to keep it warm.

Step 2

  1. Using a fork, beat 5 eggs, water, salt and turmeric in a small bowl until combined.
  2. Melt 5 teaspoons of coconut oil or ghee over medium-high heat. Tilt the pan from side to side to coat the bottom
    Pour in the egg mixture.
  3. Using a slotted or spoonula spoon, gently push cooked egg portions from edges toward the center of the pan so that
    uncooked egg mixture reaches the hot pan.
  4. Continue tilting the pan until the top surface of the eggs is thickened and no visible liquid remains.
  5. Now pour the vegetable pottage on the Omelette. Use the spoonulaor slotted spoon to fold the omelette in half. Slide omelette onto
    a serving plate and then serve immediately.
